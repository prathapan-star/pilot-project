Memory Match Game Instructions

  • Objective: Match all pairs of images on the gameboard.
  • Game Setup:
    • The gameboard consists of an 8x8 grid of cells.
    • Each cell initially displays the same image (a joystick).
    • Hidden behind these initial images are pairs of other images.
  • Gameplay:
    • Click on any cell to reveal the image hidden underneath.
    • Clicking on a cell reveals the image hidden underneath.
    • Images will remain visible after being clicked.
  • Matching:
    • When two cells are clicked consecutively, their hidden images are revealed.
    • If the two revealed images match, they remain visible, and the player earns points.
    • If the two revealed images do not match, they are hidden again after a brief delay.
  • Winning the Game:
    • Continue revealing and matching pairs of images until all pairs are matched.
    • The game is won when all pairs of images are successfully matched.
  • Game Controls:
    • Click on a cell to reveal the hidden image.
    • Remember the location of previously revealed images to make successful matches.
    • The game automatically tracks matching pairs and updates the gameboard accordingly.
  • Enjoy the Challenge:
    • Test your memory and concentration skills with this entertaining game.
    • Challenge yourself to improve your matching speed and accuracy.
    • Have fun exploring the images hidden within the gameboard!
